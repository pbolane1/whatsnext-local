updated html::ProcessTemplate and email::TemplateMail for suport of conditionals.  Conditionals basically depend on <replace/>  structure; when used in conjuntion with email templates (recommended / only intentioanlly suported applicaiton, structure for suported conditionals looks like this:)
     <if replace/>   .....   </if replace/>
     <if !replace/>   .....   </if !replace/>
     <each replace_array/>   .....   
	       <KEY/> is suported.
	       <replace_array_subvalue/> where replace_array_subvalue is an attribute of replace_array[KEY]
	 </each !replace_array/>

added email::ConvertTemplateReplacements() function in suport of nested values.
     $params['x']=array('foo'=>'bar','fa'=>'baz');
         becomes....
     $replace['<x/>']=array('foo'=>'<bar/>','fa'=>'<baz/>');

consider also suporting:
     <item[index]/>
     <item[index][attribute]/>
     <if foo='bar'/>
     <else if foo='baz'/>
     <else/>
     </if foo>
      OR: switch/cases for the above....
