Click on the plus sign on the next to the type of calendar you want to use:

+ Apple Computer

  1. Click here to copy your URL.
  2. Open the Calendar app.
  3. Go to File > New Calendar Subscription.
  4. Paste the address you copied in step 1
  5. Click Subscribe.
  6. Give the Calendar a name, optionally uncheck Remove alerts, and change auto-refresh to every day
  7. Click OK.

+ iPhone / iPad

  1. Click here to copy your URL.
  2. Open the Settings app.
  3. Go to Calendar > Accounts > Add Account > Other.
  4. Tap Add Subscribed Calendar.
  5. paste the link you copied in step 1 iinto the "Server" field.
  6. Tap Next, then configure options (e.g., name and refresh interval).
  7. Tap Save.

+ Google Calendar

  1. Click here to copy your URL.
  2. Open Google Calendar in your web browser.
  3. On the left sidebar, find "Other calendars".
  4. Click the "+" icon next to "Other calendars" and select "From URL".
  5. Paste the link you copied in Step 1
  6. Click "Add calendar".
  7. The calendar will now appear under "Other calendars" in your Google Calendar list.

+ Outlook (Desktop)

  1. Click here to copy your URL.
  2. Open Outlook on your computer
  3. Click on the calendar icon near the top left
  4. Click Add Calendar
  5. In the left panel, click "Subscribe from web"
  6. Paste the link you copied in step 1
  7. Give the calendar a name
  8. Optionally select a color and charm
  9. Optionally change where the calendar is saved by selecting a location under the "Add To" dropdown
  10. Click import

+ Outlook (Web)

  1. Click here to copy your URL.
  2. Open Outlook on the web.
  3. Near the top left, click the calendar icon
  4. Click Add Calendar.
  5. Choose "Subscribe from web"
  6. Paste the link you copied in step 1
  7. Give the calendar a name
  8. Optionally select a color and charm
  9. Optionally change where the calendar is saved by selecting a location under the "Add To" dropdown
  10. Click import

+ Thunderbird

  1. Click here to copy your URL.
  2. Open Thunderbird and go to the Calendar view.
  3. Click on the calendar icon on the left side.
  4. Click "New Calendar..." at the bottom
  5. Select "On the Network" and click Next
  6. Leave Username blank
  7. In the location field, paste in the address you copied in Step 1
  8. Check the box "This location doesn't require credentials" and leave the "Offline Support" box checked.
  9. Click Find Calendars
  10. Optionally click on the properties button to give the calendar a name, color, and refresh rate, and then click Ok.
  11. Click Subscribe

+ Yahoo Calendar

  1. Click here to copy your URL.
  2. Go to Yahoo Calendar and sign in.
  3. On the left, hover your mouse over the Others section and click the gear icon
  4. Click Follow other calendars
  5. Give the Calendar a title
  6. Paste the link you copied in step 1 into the Calendar URL field
  7. Select color, refresh interval and select an option in the Remind dropdown
  8. Click Save

+ Zoho Calendar

  1. Click here to copy your URL.
  2. Go to Zoho Calendar.
  3. In the left sidebar, click the plus icon next to My Calendars
  4. Click on Subscribe to a calendar
  5. Paste the link copied in step 1 into the box on the right
  6. Click Subscribe
